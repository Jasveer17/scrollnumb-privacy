Delete Your ScrollNumb Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your ScrollNumb account and the data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app (instant)

  1. Open ScrollNumb
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete account & data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email djsingh585@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign in, with the subject "Delete my ScrollNumb account". We will delete your account within 30 days and confirm by reply.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, username, email, gender, age, mobile number, avatar)
  • Your activity data (scroll counts, XP, streaks, analytics history)
  • Your leaderboard entry and presence record
  • Your sign-in (authentication) account
  • All data stored locally on your device

Deletion via the app is immediate and cannot be undone.

What may be kept

Anonymous crash reports and aggregated analytics that are not linked to your identity may be retained to keep the app stable. No personal data is kept after deletion.
